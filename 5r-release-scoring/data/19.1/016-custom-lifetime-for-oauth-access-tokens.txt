Title: Custom lifetime for OAuth access tokens
Release: 19.1
Source: https://docs.gitlab.com/releases/19/gitlab-19-1-released/
Tier: Free, Premium, Ultimate
Offering: GitLab Self-Managed, GitLab Dedicated
Links: Documentation · Related Issue

By default, OAuth access tokens in GitLab expire after two hours. In GitLab 19.1, instance administrators on GitLab Self-Managed
and GitLab Dedicated can set a custom lifetime for new OAuth access tokens. You can configure any value from 300 to 7200seconds. This helps you enforce shorter-lived tokens for security-sensitive OAuth integrations, including MCP clients, without
changing the behavior of existing tokens.
