Title: Custom flows YAML validation
Release: 19.1
Source: https://docs.gitlab.com/releases/19/gitlab-19-1-released/
Tier: Premium, Ultimate
Offering: GitLab.com, GitLab Self-Managed, GitLab Dedicated
Links: Documentation · Related Issue

The AI Catalog now validates your custom flow configuration before saving or triggering it.
Previously, syntax errors and misconfigured parameters in a custom flow (for example, missing inputs or unknown toolparameters) only surfaced at runtime, after a CI job had already started. This made debugging slow and difficult.
Now, when you save or update a custom flow in the AI Catalog, GitLab checks the configuration upfront and surfaces any errors
directly in the UI. Valid flows are unaffected and continue to save and trigger as usual.
