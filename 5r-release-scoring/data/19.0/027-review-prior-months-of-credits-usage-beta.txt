Title: Review prior months of Credits usage (Beta)
Release: 19.0
Source: https://docs.gitlab.com/releases/19/gitlab-19-0-released/
Tier: Premium, Ultimate
Offering: GitLab.com, GitLab Self-Managed
Links: Documentation

In previous versions of GitLab, the Credits dashboard showed only the current billing month, so you couldnʼt compare trends,
audit a usage spike after the fact, or build a budget conversation around prior consumption. Now you can navigate to prior billing
months in the Credits dashboard.
