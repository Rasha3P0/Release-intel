Title: Restrict the AI Catalog to a group hierarchy
Release: 19.0
Source: https://docs.gitlab.com/releases/19/gitlab-19-0-released/
Tier: Premium, Ultimate
Offering: GitLab.com, GitLab Self-Managed, GitLab Dedicated
Links: Documentation, Related issue

Top-level group Owners can now restrict the AI Catalog to show only agents and flows owned by projects within their group
hierarchy. This blocks agents, external agents, or flows not in this hierarchy from being visible or enabled by any user in thatgroup.
